Toronto, Canada's largest city and financial capital, anchors the Golden Horseshoe manufacturing belt — one of North America's most concentrated industrial regions. The Greater Toronto Area (GTA) and surrounding Ontario manufacturing corridor host major automotive OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers (Honda, Toyota, Magna International), food and beverage processors, chemical manufacturers, and a thriving mining equipment and services sector serving Canada's vast resource industry. This industrial diversity creates strong demand for engineering plastics across multiple performance tiers.

Nylon and POM for Canadian Automotive Manufacturing

Ontario's automotive manufacturing cluster — centred on Oshawa, Windsor, Cambridge, and Alliston — is one of North America's most productive, assembling over 1.4 million vehicles annually. Tier 1 and Tier 2 automotive suppliers throughout the GTA require precision-machined nylon (PA6, PA66, PA12), POM (Delrin), and PEEK components for under-hood applications, transmission components, fuel system parts, and structural brackets. Our engineering plastic rods, sheets, and tubes are supplied with full material traceability and are available in automotive-grade formulations with enhanced thermal stability and dimensional consistency.

UHMWPE for Canadian Food Processing

Canada's food processing industry — particularly the meat packing, dairy, and grain processing sectors in Ontario and Alberta — requires FDA and Health Canada-compliant UHMWPE and HDPE components for cutting boards, conveyor wear strips, guide rails, and processing equipment components. Our food-grade UHMWPE products are manufactured from virgin resin with no recycled content, comply with FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 and EU Regulation 10/2011, and are available in white, blue (for metal-detectable applications), and natural grades.

Ground Protection Mats for Canadian Oil Sands and Mining

Canada's oil sands operations in Fort McMurray, Alberta, and the mining operations of Ontario's Ring of Fire and Northern Ontario mineral belt require ground protection solutions for the challenging muskeg (peat bog) terrain. Our UHMWPE ground protection mats provide stable working platforms for drilling equipment, mining machinery, and construction plant on soft, waterlogged ground. The mats' cold-temperature formulation maintains flexibility and impact resistance down to -40°C, essential for Canadian winter operations.

We ship to Toronto via the Port of Montreal (for St. Lawrence Seaway access) or via the Port of Halifax with onward rail to Toronto. Standard sea freight transit time from our factory in Hebei Province, China, to Montreal is 28-35 days. Road transport from Montreal to Toronto takes approximately 6 hours. Alternatively, shipments can be routed via the Port of Vancouver with cross-Canada rail, with a total transit time of 30-38 days.

Canadian Standards and Certifications

Our products for the Canadian market comply with relevant CSA (Canadian Standards Association) standards and US ASTM equivalents recognised in Canada. Food-contact products comply with Health Canada's food safety regulations and FDA 21 CFR requirements. For mining applications, products are available with third-party test certificates from SGS Canada or Bureau Veritas Canada.

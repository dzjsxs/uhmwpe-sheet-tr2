Choosing the Right Engineering Plastic

Selecting the optimal engineering plastic for your application requires understanding the specific performance requirements, environmental conditions, and cost constraints. This guide covers the most important factors.

Material Comparison

UHMWPE (Ultra-High Molecular Weight Polyethylene)

Best for: Wear applications, impact resistance, chemical environments. Available from UHMWPE levha üreticisi Çin.

  • Highest wear resistance among commodity plastics
  • Self-lubricating, no maintenance required
  • Excellent chemical resistance
  • Operating range: -200°C to +80°C

Nylon (PA6, PA66)

Best for: Gears, bearings, bushings, structural parts requiring good mechanical properties.

HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene)

Best for: Cost-effective wear applications, food-grade requirements, marine environments.

PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene)

Best for: Lowest friction applications, high temperature (up to 260°C), aggressive chemicals.

Quick Selection Guide

Wear/abrasion → UHMWPE | High temp → PEEK/PTFE | Precision → POM | Cost-effective → HDPE/Nylon | Food grade → UHMWPE/HDPE

Application-Specific Recommendations

For UHMWPE levha üreticisi applications, our engineering team recommends UHMWPE for most wear applications, with nylon for load-bearing components and HDPE for cost-sensitive projects.

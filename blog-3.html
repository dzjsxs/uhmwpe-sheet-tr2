The Business Case for Engineering Plastics

Companies switching from metal to engineering plastic components consistently report significant cost savings and reliability improvements. Here are the key drivers of ROI.

Cost Reduction Factors

1. Extended Service Life

UHMWPE wear components last 3-7× longer than steel equivalents in abrasive applications. This directly reduces replacement frequency and associated labor costs.

2. Reduced Maintenance Downtime

Self-lubricating properties eliminate lubrication maintenance. Lighter weight components are faster to replace when needed. Non-stick surfaces prevent material buildup that requires manual clearing.

3. Lower Total Weight

Engineering plastics weigh 7-8× less than steel. This reduces structural load requirements and can enable equipment redesign for improved efficiency.

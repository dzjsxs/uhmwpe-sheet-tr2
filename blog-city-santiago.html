Santiago, Chile's capital and industrial hub, serves as the command centre for the world's largest copper mining industry and a rapidly growing lithium sector. Chile produces approximately 27% of the world's copper and holds the world's largest lithium reserves in the Atacama Desert. The mining operations of Codelco, BHP Escondida, Anglo American Los Bronces, and dozens of other operators create an immense demand for wear-resistant engineering plastics capable of withstanding the abrasive, high-tonnage conditions of Chilean mineral processing.

Polyurethane Screen Panels for Chilean Copper Processing

Copper ore processing in Chile's major mining districts — Atacama, Antofagasta, and O'Higgins — relies heavily on vibrating screen technology for ore sizing, dewatering, and concentrate classification. Our polyurethane screen panels are engineered specifically for the high-silica, abrasive copper ores of the Andes, with Shore A hardness options from 50A to 85A and custom aperture geometries to match each operation's particle size distribution. We supply panels compatible with all major screen OEM frame systems including Metso, Sandvik, Derrick, and Schenck.

UHMWPE Wear Liners for Copper Concentrate Handling

The handling of copper concentrate — from the concentrator plant to the port at Antofagasta, Mejillones, or Iquique — involves extensive conveyor, chute, and hopper systems that are subject to severe abrasive wear. UHMWPE wear liners installed in transfer chutes, loading spouts, and storage bins dramatically reduce maintenance intervals and extend equipment life. Our UHMWPE liners are available in standard and custom profiles, with thicknesses from 10mm to 100mm and surface textures optimised for smooth material flow.

Lithium Mining Applications in the Atacama

Chile's lithium brine operations in the Salar de Atacama — operated by SQM and Albemarle — present unique engineering challenges due to the extreme salinity, UV radiation, and temperature cycling of the high-altitude desert environment. Our UV-stabilised HDPE and UHMWPE products are formulated for long-term performance in these conditions. HDPE geomembrane-grade sheet is used for evaporation pond liners, while UHMWPE components are specified for brine pump wear parts and pipeline systems.

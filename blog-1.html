Introduction to UHMWPE Engineering Plastics

Ultra-High Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE) is the premier engineering plastic for wear-intensive industrial applications. With molecular weights exceeding 3.5 million g/mol, it delivers performance that surpasses steel in many applications.

As a leading manufacturer, UHMWPE levha üreticisi Çin supplies UHMWPE sheets, rods, and custom machined parts to industries worldwide.

Key Properties

  • Wear resistance: 6× better than mild steel
  • Self-lubricating surface (COF: 0.10–0.22)
  • Impact strength: 10× higher than nylon 66
  • Chemical resistance: excellent against acids, alkalis, solvents
  • Operating temperature: -200°C to +80°C
  • FDA approved for food contact

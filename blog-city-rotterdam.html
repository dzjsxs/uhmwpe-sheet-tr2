Rotterdam, home to Europe's largest port and one of the world's most sophisticated petrochemical complexes, represents the gateway to European industrial supply chains. The Port of Rotterdam handles over 440 million tonnes of cargo annually, while the Rotterdam-Moerdijk industrial cluster hosts the European operations of Shell, BP, ExxonMobil, LyondellBasell, and dozens of other major chemical and energy companies. The combination of world-class port infrastructure, a dense petrochemical cluster, and proximity to European automotive and food processing industries makes Rotterdam one of our most strategically important markets.

UHMWPE Marine Fenders for Rotterdam's Port Infrastructure

The Port of Rotterdam's 42km of quays, 12 port districts, and 5 major container terminals (ECT Delta, ECT Euromax, APM Terminals Maasvlakte II, and others) require a continuous supply of UHMWPE marine fender pads, dock bumpers, and berthing systems. Rotterdam's tidal range and the size of the vessels calling at the port — including the world's largest container ships and VLCCs — demand fender systems with exceptional impact absorption and wear resistance. Our UHMWPE fender products conform to PIANC guidelines and are available with Lloyd's Register, Bureau Veritas, or DNV GL material certificates.

HDPE and PP for Rotterdam's Petrochemical Complex

The Rotterdam-Moerdijk petrochemical cluster — processing over 100 million tonnes of crude oil and chemical feedstocks annually — requires extensive HDPE and PP piping, tank liners, and process equipment components for chemical storage and transfer applications. Our HDPE pipe-grade materials meet EN 12201 and ISO 4427 standards, while our PP-H (homopolymer) and PP-R (random copolymer) products conform to EN ISO 15494 for industrial piping systems. For aggressive chemical service, we supply PVDF (Kynar) sheet and pipe for applications involving concentrated acids, halogens, and organic solvents.

PTFE and PEEK for High-Performance Process Applications

Rotterdam's refinery and chemical plant operators require PTFE and PEEK components for the most demanding process conditions — high temperatures, aggressive chemicals, and high pressures. Our PTFE products conform to DIN EN ISO 12086 and are available in virgin, glass-filled, carbon-filled, and bronze-filled grades. PEEK components are available in unfilled and 30% carbon-filled grades for continuous service up to 250°C. We supply valve seats, pump impellers, gaskets, and custom-machined components to customer drawings.

EU Standards and REACH Compliance

All our products for the European market comply with EU REACH regulation (EC 1907/2006) and relevant EN ISO standards. Material safety data sheets (SDS) are available in Dutch, English, German, and French. CE marking is available for construction products. Our food-contact products comply with EU Regulation 10/2011 and Dutch NVWA food safety requirements.
